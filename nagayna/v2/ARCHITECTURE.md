## The 12 modules

1. **User Interface** вҖ” the panel where the user sees assets, charts, signals, recommendations, risk, trades, PnL, journal, news, reports.

2. **Exchange Connector** вҖ” connects to Bybit, Coinbase, Kraken, KuCoin, OKX, and Binance (if available). Starts read-only. See [INTEGRATIONS.md](./INTEGRATIONS.md#exchanges).

3. **TradingView Connector** вҖ” receives signals: webhooks, alerts, Pine Script strategies, user indicators. See [INTEGRATIONS.md](./INTEGRATIONS.md#tradingview).

4. **Market Data Engine** вҖ” collects candles, volumes, order book, funding, open interest, liquidations, volatility.

5. **Indicator Engine** вҖ” computes the technical indicators. See [INDICATORS.md](./INDICATORS.md).

6. **AI Reasoning Engine** вҖ” explains the situation and forms a trade idea, weighing indicator groups, multi-timeframe, market context, and news.

7. **Memory Engine** вҖ” stores the history of decisions, trades, and conclusions. See [LEARNING.md](./LEARNING.md).

8. **Backtesting Engine** вҖ” tests strategies on history.

9. **Paper Trading Engine** вҖ” trains the system with no money.

10. **Risk Engine** вҖ” limits risk and acts as the final gate on every trade. See [RISK_ENGINE.md](./RISK_ENGINE.md).

11. **News Intelligence Engine** вҖ” scans news and events, filtered by importance. See [INTEGRATIONS.md](./INTEGRATIONS.md#news-intelligence).

12. **Report Engine** вҖ” creates daily, weekly, and monthly reports.

---

## Data flow in one line

Market data + TradingView signals + news вҶ’ Indicator Engine вҶ’ AI Reasoning Engine forms an idea вҶ’ **Risk Engine gates it** вҶ’ (backtest / paper / confirmation) вҶ’ outcome recorded in Memory вҶ’ Report Engine summarizes вҶ’ self-improvement cycle. See [LEARNING.md](./LEARNING.md#the-self-improvement-cycle).
