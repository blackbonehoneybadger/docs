## Variables Affecting Performance

### Market Conditions
- Bull markets: Performance enhanced
- Bear markets: Performance reduced
- Sideways markets: Lower returns expected

### Volatility
- High volatility: Larger swings, more opportunities
- Low volatility: Tighter stops, more frequent whipsaws

### Liquidity
- High liquidity (BTC/ETH): Consistent execution
- Low liquidity (altcoins): Slippage increases, profits reduced

### Regulation
- Favorable regulation: Price support, adoption
- Unfavorable regulation: Price pressure, restrictions
