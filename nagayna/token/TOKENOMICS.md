## Token Mechanics

### Burn Mechanism

- **Trade Fee Burn**: 1% of all platform trades are burned
- **Purpose**: Increase scarcity, incentivize long-term holding
- **Annual Burn Target**: 5-10% of total supply (estimated)
- **Duration**: Perpetual until supply reaches 100M tokens

**Example**: If $100M in trading volume occurs annually:
- Assuming 0.5% avg token per trade = ~500,000 tokens burned per year
- This creates continuous deflationary pressure

### Utility Functions

NGY token is used for:

1. **Trading Fees** — 0.1% discount when paying fees in NGY
2. **Governance** — Vote on protocol changes and fund allocation
3. **Staking** — Earn rewards proportional to stake
4. **Access** — Unlock premium features and strategy signals
5. **Liquidity** — Provide liquidity on DEXs for rewards
6. **Rebalancing** — Purchase and reinvest profits into the bot
